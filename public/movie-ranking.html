My Best 5 Movie Recommendations

Here are my favorite movies of all time.

Avengers: Infinity War

Infinity War breaks the mold of traditional superhero movies by placing the villain, Thanos, at the center of the narrative.

The Call

The Call is a movie that thrives on its effective use of mysterious atmosphere.

Up

Up is a story about aging and realizing that life is messy and not something that we can always control.

Cars

My childhood movie.

Avengers: Endgame

Delivering a climax suited to one of the most incredible sagas to ever hit our screens.
